# Lighting Design Notes — piece briefs

Briefs from Lionel for the lighting designs to be built. Source of truth for the
creative direction of each piece's lighting.

## Standard pieces
1. **Fantasie Impromptu** — dark, moody, netherworld; purple and blue.
2. **Prelude in G Minor** — red, warfare, epic, warlike.
3. **Rolling Thunder** — stormy, thunder (small flashes of light for thunder, but not
   too much); blue and white.
4. **Fight for Freedom** — orange and red, heroic, intensity, flames.
5. **Colors of the Soul** — kaleidoscope of colors, leaning towards yellow and green
   (colors we haven't really used yet).

## Nightmare pieces
Most start with a NON-nightmare version (~2 min), then ~2 min nightmare.

1. **Torrent Etude** (reg version): calm white. **Nightmare:** white and yellow with
   flashes of purple, stormy. (We already have a lighting plot for this — build off of it.)
2. **Fur Elise** (reg version): romantic, pink and red. **Nightmare:** purple, red,
   romantic but tinged with darkness.
3. **Canon in Dream** (EDM entire time): regal, orchestral, heroic orange with purple tints.
4. **Moonlight Sonata** (reg version): blue, dark moonlight. **Nightmare:** dark blue,
   purple, electric pulses of light.

## General direction
- Aim for more movement, more color fixtures, more fading in and out. **MAKE IT DYNAMIC.**
- Build #1 (Fantasie Impromptu) first for review.
